Unit Quick Sheet
All HP and UP values are listed in Green / Regular / Veteran order.
InfantryMove 1HP 3/4/5UP 3/5/7Front line. Melee: 2 dice. Entering hills ends movement; woods/hills/rough also apply next-turn pause.
CavalryMove 2HP 2/3/4UP 6/8/10Shock arm. Melee: 3 dice. Lightning strike: move 1 to attack, then disengage 1 if movement remains (not if already engaged from being attacked first). Woods/rough entries cause pause. Cannot climb hills unless already on hills.
SkirmisherMove 2HP 1/2/3UP 2/3/4Screen unit. Melee: 1 die. Ranged: 1 die at 2 hexes. May jump over adjacent friendly Infantry to land two hexes away if open. Woods: one hex per activation. Hills: full speed.
ArcherMove 1HP 1/2/3UP 2/4/6Ranged pressure. Melee: 1 die. Ranged: 2 dice at 2 hexes, 1 die at 3. Woods entry causes next-turn pause; entering hills ends movement.
GeneralMove 2HP 2/3/4UP 8/10/12Command anchor. Melee: 1 die. Sets command radius. Woods: one hex per activation.
RunnerMove 3/4/5HP 2/2/2UP 1/2/3No attack. Carries command relay (radius 1). Woods/hills: one hex per activation.
MedicMove 1HP 1/1/1UP 4/4/4No attack. Heals +1 HP to one adjacent ally; healed unit is spent for the turn. Woods entry causes next-turn pause.
Dice: 5-6 hit, 4 retreat, 1-3 miss. Defender modifiers can reduce attacker dice, but attacks always roll at least 1 die. Open Rules for full detail.
Terrain Guide
Clear
Move: All units cost 1.
Combat: No direct combat modifier.
Woods
Move: INF/ARC/MED/CAV: entering woods applies next-turn pause. RUN/SKR/GEN: one woods hex per activation (no woods pause).
Combat: Defender applies attacker −1 die. Archers and skirmishers can fire only from woods that touch clear hexes (tree-line).
Hills
Move: INF/ARC/GEN can climb, but entering hills ends movement. SKR climb at full speed. RUN move one hill hex per activation. CAV cannot climb onto hills unless already starting on hills. MED cannot enter.
Combat: Archers/skirmishers on hills gain +1 ranged die. Attacking those units on hills gives attacker −1 die.
Rough
Move: Most units can enter. Cavalry and generals are slower, and all units that enter rough pause next turn. Medics cannot enter.
Combat: Any attack launched from rough is −1 die. Entering rough applies next-turn full inactivity (unit cannot activate).
Water
Move: Impassable.
Combat: Cannot enter.
