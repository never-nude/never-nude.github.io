How ThreadKeep works

ThreadKeep is designed for people who already use Messages on their Mac and want an easier way to revisit older conversations.

1

Bring in your Messages

Choose all your conversations or only the ones you want to keep handy.

2

Browse them like a library

See names, dates, and conversations in one place instead of scrolling forever in Messages.

3

Find what you forgot

Search for places, links, plans, and details you know are in a conversation somewhere.

ThreadKeep is a Mac-first product. This page is a preview hosted on GitHub Pages at /threadkeep/.
